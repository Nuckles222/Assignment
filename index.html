This is an D-Dice Roller, Allowing you to Roll from Between an D2 to an D100.
all that is needed is the choice of the dice to roll. You can also Choose How Many rolls of the dice you want.
Example: you want to roll an D12 (12 Sided dice) 6 times, it would then give you the results for 6 times a virtual D12 was rolled
